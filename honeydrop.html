Our Story

Born from a love of golden mornings

Honey Drip began as a dream scribbled on a napkin — a café that felt like sunlight through a jar of honey. Warm, golden, unhurried.

Every item on our menu is crafted with the same philosophy: use the best, keep it honest, make it something people want to come back for on their worst days.
